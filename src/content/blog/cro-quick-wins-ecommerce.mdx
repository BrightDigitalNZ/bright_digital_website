You do not need a redesign to improve conversion rates. These 12 changes can be implemented this week and will move the needle on your e-commerce revenue.

**1.** Add urgency to product pages (stock levels, limited-time pricing).

**2.** Reduce form fields to the absolute minimum.

**3.** Add trust signals next to your CTA (reviews, guarantees, security badges).

**4.** Make your CTA button a contrasting colour and above the fold.

**5.** Add a sticky add-to-cart bar on mobile.

**6.** Show shipping costs early, not at checkout.

**7.** Use real customer photos alongside professional product shots.

**8.** Add a progress bar to your checkout flow.

**9.** Offer guest checkout (do not force account creation).

**10.** Add live chat or a chatbot to high-intent pages.

**11.** Test your page load speed and fix anything over 3 seconds.

**12.** Add exit-intent popups with a relevant offer (not a generic discount).

Each of these changes can improve conversion rates by 2-10%. Stack a few together and the compound effect is significant.
