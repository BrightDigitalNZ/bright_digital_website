If your business serves customers in a specific area, local SEO is the most cost-effective way to get found. When someone in Auckland searches “SEO agency near me” or “digital marketing Tauranga”, local SEO determines whether your business appears in the map pack and local results.

## Google Business Profile

Your GBP is the foundation of local SEO. Complete every field, add photos regularly, post updates weekly and respond to every review. Businesses with complete, active profiles rank significantly higher in local results.

## Local citations

Get listed on NZ-specific directories: Yellow, Localist, NZ Business Directory, and industry-specific directories relevant to your sector. Consistent NAP (name, address, phone) across all listings is critical.

## Reviews

Volume, recency and rating all matter. Build a systematic process for asking happy customers to leave Google reviews. Respond to every review, positive and negative.

## Local content

Create pages targeting your service areas: “SEO services Auckland”, “digital marketing Christchurch”, “Google Ads agency Wellington”. Each page should have unique, valuable content specific to that location.
